Who am I?

Born in Mexico, I moved to France at the age of 18 to pursue my passion for helping people through health technologies.

Since I was young, I have been fascinated by the potential of technology, especially in the medical field. Driven by a passion to help people, I am dedicated to leveraging advanced technology to revolutionize healthcare, aiming to improve medical imaging and diagnostics with intelligent systems.

Initially intrigued by the prospect of becoming a medical physician with the bachelor's in TechSan, I soon realized that direct medical practice was not my calling. Instead, I redirected my focus towards coding and technology, continuing my education with a master's degree in image processing and computer vision. This shift proved to be the best decision I've ever made, allowing me to blend my technical skills with my desire to make a significant impact in healthcare.

Now, I am a student advancing my skills in C++ and Python, optimization, and data science through my master's program. My studies focus on deep learning, electronics and robotics, equipping me with the knowledge to innovate and improve intelligent systems. I am passionate about leveraging technology in real-world applications and am actively involved in projects that enhance my expertise in these areas.
